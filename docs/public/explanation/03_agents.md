# The Agents of Voltaire

State: **Active**
Version: **1.0** (Phase 4 Verified)

The Voltaire system is composed of specialized autonomous agents, each with a distinct persona and domain layout.

## 1. Simons (The Physicist)

* **Domain**: **Reflex Kernel (L1/L2)**
* **Role**: Quantifies the "Physics" of price action.
* **Tools**: Feynman Mechanics ($v, a, j$), Taleb Statistics ($\Omega$).
* **Philosophy**: "Markets are physical systems subject to fluid dynamics."

## 2. Hypatia (The Librarian)

* **Domain**: **The Brain (L2)**
* **Role**: Understands the "Why" (Semantics/Narrative).
* **Tools**: DistilBERT (Sentiment), LanceDB (Memory/Regime Recall).
* **Philosophy**: "History rhymes. Context is everything."

## 3. Boyd (The Fighter Pilot)

* **Domain**: **Execution (L4)**
* **Role**: Executes the will of the Governor with precision.
* **Tools**: Order Gateway, Latency Optimization.
* **Philosophy**: "Speed and accuracy. OODA loops win dogfights."

## 4. The Governor (The Sovereign)

* **Domain**: **Governance (L3)**
* **Role**: The final arbiter of Truth and Safety.
* **Tools**: OODA Loop, Veto Gate, Provisional Executive.
* **Philosophy**: "Safety > Profit. Survive to play another round."
